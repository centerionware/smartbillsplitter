Your Data is Yours. By Design.

We built SharedBills on a "privacy first" principle. Your data lives on your device, giving you full control. The app is fast, secure, and works completely offline.

When you choose to share a bill, a temporary, end-to-end encrypted copy passes through our server. We cannot read this data. Only the person you send the unique, secure link to can decrypt and view the bill's details. Your privacy is never compromised.
